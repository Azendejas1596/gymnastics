Gymnastics Webpage

Created By: Ally Zendejas

Hello! My Name is Ally Zendejas. This is a webpage I have created that is dedicated to my love of gymnastics. I was a competitive gymnast for three years, and did recreation gymnastics since I was three years old! Gymnastics was a wonderful part of my life, and made many acomplishments such as being named the 2017 North Carolina State Champion. Although I thought I would always be a gymnast, at the end of my third year on the competitive team I had a severe injury which caused me to be out of the sport for several months. Once I was fully recovered I talked to my doctors and we decided that it would be too risky for me to go back. At the time myself, my parents, coaches, and teamates were hearbroken, but now I can see that it was for the best. The lfie skills I earned from gymnastics are everlasting and a part of the reason I am the person who I am today. I am so thankful for my gymnastics journey and still have a huge apprecciation for the sport. I hope you enjoy my webpage and learn a few things about this wonderful sport!
